# Can you Survived 10 Days to Payday

A simple choice-based financial game developed using Godot. This game has 4 mini games inside that you can play!

#GAMES
1. Choice-based financial: Navigate through various scenarios where your financial decisions impact your budget and your future.
2. Payday check: Players experience the relief of receiving their income while instantly managing basic needs, bills, and savings.
3. Inflation: Where market prices rise over time.
4. Smartshopper: A mini-game that tests your ability to distinguish between Needs vs. Wants, identify discount traps, and understand opportunity costs.
